Welcome!

I’m Roan, an Astronomy PhD student at the University of Nottingham. I work with Meghan Gray and Frazer Pearce, and my research focuses on cosmological simulations of galaxy clusters.

As well as research, I am involved in science outreach — I manage the University of Nottingham’s inflatable planetarium, which we use to present planetarium shows to schools in the local area. I have also given science talks to wider audiences, and I organise weekly talks in the Nottingham Astronomy Department. Please get in touch if you have questions about any of these things!
